About the Speaker

ABOUT SPEAKER:
Sonu Kaur is a research scholar in the area of Human Resource Development and Associate Professor in the department of Business Administration and Management. She is an expert in the area of Human Resource Management and Business Development with long public standing. The diversity of her vast experience as – educator, motivational speaker and analyst has been the crowning glory of her vast profession since last 22 years. She is a very prolific writer par at excellence and has covered very wide ranging social and managerial issues concerning with human life. She is extremely receptive, amenable, pleasing and adaptable to changing environment. She has a very deep penetrating insight into human relationship and its multitude problems being faced particularly by our youths in India and at global level too. She possesses very charming, dynamic, amenable, and attractive personality. She is blessed with very versatile talent, and has very rare kind of caliber to solve the human problems of complex nature. She is not only a writer but also is an erudite speaker, fearless public orator, and a keen debater with dauntless courage and conviction. She has an excellent vision and remarkable managerial capabilities of high quality. She is gifted with creativity, originality in thoughts, and childlike innocence and simplicity devoid of any spite and malice sprit. She is an inspirational speaker who delivers heartfelt speeches with the intention of encouraging or inspiring people to live their lives to the fullest and to help them make some kind of professional or personal change in their lives and most importantly within themselves. She has an ardent love in acquisition and dissemination of knowledge to make life more ameliorable and worth living.
